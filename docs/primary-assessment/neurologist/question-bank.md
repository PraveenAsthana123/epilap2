# Neurologist — Hospital-Grade Question Bank (categorised)

> **Why (this doc):** The full categorised neurologist consultation questionnaire — Chief Complaint,
> Seizure History, Aura & Symptoms, Triggers, Medication, Past Medical/Family/Social history, and
> Examination — the hospital‑grade expansion behind the Neurologist role. **How:** one block per
> category with exact IDs. A **filled dataset for 60 patients** (these questions + EEG001–080
> answered) is generated by `analysis/generate_qbank_dataset.py` → `data/analysis/
> neurologist_answers.csv` + `eeg_technician_answers.csv` (viewable in the viewer **Data** tab).

## Chief Complaint (CC001–CC025)
What brings you in today · biggest concern · when it began · duration · sudden/gradual onset ·
improving/worsening · severity · most concerning symptom · continuous/intermittent · frequency ·
what happens during an episode · episode duration · pre‑episode symptoms · during‑episode symptoms ·
post‑episode symptoms · triggers · aggravating factors · relieving factors · impact on work/school ·
impact on daily activities · ED visits · hospital admissions · driving impact · family concerns · goals for today.

## Seizure History (SH001–SH030)

seizures/day · /week · /month · /year · shortest duration · usual duration · longest duration · last
seizure · seizure‑free days · frequency increasing · severity increasing · nocturnal · awake · time of
day · menstrual relation · stereotypy · clusters · cluster size · status epilepticus · SE episodes ·
rescue medication used · rescue drug · falls · fractures/injuries · overall control past year.

## Aura & Clinical Symptoms (AS001–AS025)

olfactory · gustatory · tingling/numbness · dizziness · fear/anxiety · déjà vu/jamais vu · palpitations ·
sweating · loss of awareness · eye deviation · automatisms · stiffening/jerking · lateralised · post‑ictal
confusion · confusion duration · Todd's paralysis · dysphasia · time to baseline.

## Trigger Assessment (TR001–TR025)

## Medication History & Treatment Response (MED001–MED025)

in 30 days · reason for missing · reminders used · improved since starting · % reduction · seizure‑free
≥12 mo · side effects · which side effects · severity · caused stop/reduce · prior ASMs tried · list prior +
reasons · allergy to ASM · other‑condition meds · OTC/herbal/supplements · pregnancy/contraception

## Lifestyle, Social History & Safety (LS001–LS025)
